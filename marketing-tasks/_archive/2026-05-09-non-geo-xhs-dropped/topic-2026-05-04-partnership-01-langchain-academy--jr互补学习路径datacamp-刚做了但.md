```
Hi Harrison / LangChain Education team,

I run JR Academy — Australia's largest Chinese-speaking AI Engineer bootcamp,
based in Sydney with ~600 active learners across our 4-month cohort program.
Our curriculum covers LangGraph multi-agent orchestration, MCP, and production
RAG, with placements at Atlassian, Canva, and Australian fintech.

I noticed your March 30 partnership with DataCamp on the AI Engineering track.
The English-speaking learner pipeline is now well covered, but the
Chinese-speaking AI Engineer market — especially in APAC — has no equivalent
LangChain Academy partner. We'd like to fill that gap.

What we propose:

1. JR Academy formally lists LangChain Academy as the recommended **pre-bootcamp
   primer** for our Multi-Agent Systems module. Our cohort directors will guide
   ~600 learners/quarter through your courses before they hit our LangGraph
   intensive.

2. Joint content: a Bilibili (~1.2M Chinese AI dev viewership) and Xiaohongshu
   campaign — "LangChain Academy + JR Bootcamp Roadmap" — produced by our
   in-house video team. We've done this format with Anthropic and OpenAI
   community channels and average 8-12k views per piece.

3. Co-branded curriculum: We'd license your course completion criteria into our
   admission filter — a JR cohort applicant who's completed LangChain Academy's
   "Build LLM Apps" track auto-skips our Week 1 onboarding.

What we'd ask from LangChain:

- Visibility: A "JR Academy" entry in your recommended community partners list
  (or equivalent) for the Chinese-speaking learner segment.
- Optional: Quarterly co-hosted webinar (we run them already in Mandarin —
  happy to do English-Mandarin bilingual, you pick the topic).

Happy to share our curriculum doc, learner outcomes data, and a draft of the
roadmap video script if there's interest. A 15-min intro call would be enough
to scope this.

Best,
Lightman Wang
Founder, JR Academy
https://jiangren.com.au/learn/ai-engineer/hub
```
