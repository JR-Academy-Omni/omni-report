**主题**：JR Academy: Advanced Practicum Partner for Microsoft AI Skilling Initiative (Australia)

```
Dear [Name],

Congratulations on the 3-million-Australian AI skilling commitment announced
April 23. We've been tracking the Microsoft × TAFE rollout closely and want
to propose a complementary "after-foundation" practicum partnership.

Quick context — JR Academy:
- Sydney-based AI Engineer bootcamp, 4-month cohort, ~600 active learners
- 67% of incoming learners come from prior foundation training (Coursera,
  university short courses, employer L&D programs)
- Curriculum: production RAG, LangGraph multi-agent, MCP, AI Engineer
  career placement (Atlassian, Canva, Australian fintech)

The gap we observe: TAFE / Microsoft AI Foundations graduates often come to
us reporting "I learned what AI is, I want to actually build something I can
ship to my team." Currently no formal handoff path exists between foundation
and applied bootcamp tracks.

Proposal:

1. JR Academy as a **listed advanced practicum partner** in your TAFE
   AI Foundations completion → "next step" recommendation list. We'd offer:
   - 50% scholarship for verified TAFE AI Foundations completers (cohort 2026)
   - Quarterly cohort reports back to Microsoft AU on placement outcomes
   - Co-branded landing: "Completed TAFE AI Foundations? Here's what week 1
     of a real AI Engineering cohort looks like" with case studies

2. **Joint webinar series** (quarterly, recorded for re-use):
   "From AI Foundations to AI Engineering: real projects from real cohorts"
   — TAFE / Microsoft instructors + JR cohort directors panel format

3. **Content series on JR website + LinkedIn**:
   "Finished TAFE AI Foundations? 3 steps to your first real AI project"
   — runs starting next month regardless of partnership; happy to brand
   it Microsoft-attributed if approved

What we'd ask:
- Listed partner status (no exclusivity required)
- Permission to reference "Microsoft AI Skilling Initiative completer pathway"
  in our marketing copy
- Optional: A 30-min intro call between your team and our curriculum lead

Happy to share JR's accreditation status (TEQSA-aligned), placement data,
and learner demographic profile on request.

Best,
Lightman Wang
Founder, JR Academy | Sydney
https://jiangren.com.au
```
