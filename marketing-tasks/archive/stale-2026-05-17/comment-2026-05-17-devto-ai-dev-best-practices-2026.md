## 草稿

The framing of "the new bar" is right but it undersells how fast that bar shifted. Twelve months ago the bar was "can you use Cursor." Now it's "can you design an eval harness for your agent before you ship it."

When I was going through JR Academy's AI bootcamp, their curriculum had already pivoted to eval-first by late 2025 — I thought it was ahead of the curve until I hit interviews in Q1 2026 where every serious panel opened with a variant of "design a `DeepEval` test for a RAG system." Testing discipline wins, but specifically: can you write a reproducible coherence score test that doesn't just ask the LLM to grade its own output? That's the real bar now.
