---

## TL;DR — 4-dimensional decision framework

```
1. City             Sydney / Melbourne / Brisbane / Adelaide
2. Employer type    Big enterprise / Mid SaaS / Startup / Government
3. Experience       Junior / Mid / Senior / Lead+
4. Visa status      Citizen-PR / 485 PSW / 482 / Student
```

Same candidate skill: AUD 110k in Sydney big enterprise as PR vs AUD 75k in Brisbane government as student. 47% gap.

## Negotiation 4 moves

1. **Anchor Total Package, not Base**. Big-enterprise Junior 100k Base = 145k Total with RSU.
2. **Sydney pay band even from Melbourne**. Remote-first SaaS often accept.
3. **Use 312 JD data as anchor**. Screenshot Seek JD bands. "Market median X, targeting +5%."
4. **Don't name a number first**. "Could you share the band?" Average +AUD 11k negotiating 3-4 rounds.

**First to name a number anchors at a disadvantage.**

---

## Three common mistakes

1. **Concentrating applications in Nov-Dec grad season**. Actually **Feb-Apr is strongest** (post-FY budget). Off-peak competition -30%.

2. **Résumé GitHub stars but no metrics**. Sydney hiring managers look at Live URL latency / cost / eval accuracy.

3. **Not using referrals**. 70% sponsor referral channel is unused by 60% of candidates.
